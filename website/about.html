About Us

Rongala Bharati Seva Foundation is a public charitable trust dedicated to improving the quality of life for the elderly, differently-abled, and under-served communities. We operate strictly on a not-for-profit basis, ensuring that every donation directly supports our mission.

Our foundation was established with the vision of creating an inclusive society where everyone has access to quality healthcare, rehabilitation services, and support systems. We believe in the power of community and work tirelessly to bridge gaps in healthcare and social services.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive healthcare, rehabilitation, and support services to vulnerable populations, while promoting awareness and research in public health.
