About Us

Bharati Seva Foundation is a public charitable trust dedicated to improving the quality of life for the elderly, differently-abled, and under-served communities in rural Vishakhapatnam region and surrounding South Indian villages. We operate strictly on a not-for-profit basis, ensuring that every donation directly supports our mission.

Our foundation was established with the vision of creating an inclusive society where everyone has access to quality healthcare, rehabilitation services, and support systems. We believe in the power of community compassion and work tirelessly to bridge gaps in healthcare and social services in rural areas where such services are critically lacking.
