Transforming Lives in Rural South India

Serving Vishakhapatnam region and surrounding communities with compassion and dignity

We are a public charitable trust operating strictly on a not-for-profit basis. Your support enables medical aid, day-care & rehabilitation, mobile healthcare to remote villages, awareness campaigns, public health research, and inclusive living initiatives for the elderly, differently-abled, and under-served communities of rural South India.
